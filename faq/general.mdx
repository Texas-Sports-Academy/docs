Texas Sports Academy Online is built to serve a wide range of learners and families — not just elite athletes. Whether you're exploring the program for the first time or working through a specific question before enrolling, the answers below cover the most common topics families ask about.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What grades does Texas Sports Academy Online serve?">
    Texas Sports Academy Online serves students in grades 3 through 12, roughly ages 8 through 18.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does my child have to be an athlete?">
    Not at all. The program works for any motivated learner. The 2-hour academic day leaves the rest of the day free — making it a great fit for students passionate about art, music, gaming, entrepreneurship, or any other pursuit. Many families join purely for the academics and the schedule flexibility, with no athletic involvement whatsoever.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my child keep training with their current club or travel team?">
    Yes. Because Texas Sports Academy Online is a fully online school, your child's club, travel team, or league schedule doesn't change at all. There's no conflict with existing training commitments, tournaments, or travel.

    One important note: as a private school, students enrolled in Texas Sports Academy Online are **not eligible to participate in UIL (University Interscholastic League) sports**. Your child can compete in club, AAU, travel leagues, and most homeschool leagues — confirm eligibility with your specific league director before registering.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My child isn't thriving at their current school. Will this work?">
    The personalized system is specifically designed for this situation. Every student begins with diagnostic placement tests to identify their actual knowledge level in each subject. The software then meets them exactly where they are — not where their age says they should be. Students who are behind catch up quickly because there's no wasted time on material they already know or aren't yet ready for.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Does the program work for gifted students?">
    Absolutely. Advanced students start at their actual level and move as fast as they want — there's no class pace to wait for. Students can cover two grade levels in a subject in a single year. The system keeps each student in the 80–85% accuracy sweet spot, which means they're always appropriately challenged without being overwhelmed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My child has a learning disability or IEP. Can they enroll?">
    The personalized software adapts to each student's level, 1:1 tutoring is available on demand, and the flexible schedule works well around therapy appointments. Students with ADHD, dyslexia, high-functioning autism, and general learning differences often thrive in this environment.

    That said, for students who need a physical adult presence throughout the school day, this program may not be the right fit. [Join a live webinar](https://sportsacademy.school/home/events) to discuss your child's specific needs directly with the team.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my child graduate early?">
    Yes. The program is self-paced and mastery-based. Students who complete all graduation requirements ahead of schedule can graduate early — there's no arbitrary timeline holding them back.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do both parents need to be home?">
    No. You don't need to be home teaching or supervising your child throughout the day. Live Guide sessions provide structure during the academic block, the Waste Meter detects disengagement in real time, and the parent dashboard gives you a full picture of your child's progress. Many families with two full-time working parents are successfully enrolled.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can my child go back to public school?">
    Yes. Texas Sports Academy Online is fully Cognia accredited and maintains official transcripts, so your child can return to public school at any time. The receiving school district makes final decisions about which credits transfer and how they apply.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if my child wants to leave the program?">
    There's no penalty and no contract. Email [online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com) to withdraw at any time. The MacBook must be returned, and return shipping is covered. If your family is on the voucher, any unused ESA funds remain tied to your child for future approved educational expenses.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Alpha School and how is it related?">
    Texas Sports Academy Online is part of the Alpha School family. Alpha School has been in operation for over 11 years, with a flagship campus in Austin priced at $40,000 per year. Texas Sports Academy Online uses the same 2 Hour Learning academic system that powers Alpha School. Alpha School graduates have been accepted to Stanford, Vanderbilt, NYU, UT Austin, and other top universities.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is MacKenzie Price?">
    MacKenzie Price (she/her) is the founder of Alpha School and the creator of the 2 Hour Learning academic system. She has led Alpha School for over 11 years.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My child plays cheerleading. Is that supported?">
    Yes. The program is sport-agnostic and welcomes students from any athletic or extracurricular background. Texas Sports Academy is also launching a dedicated cheer campus in Lago Vista for Fall 2026.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there an assessment every year?">
    Yes. Texas Sports Academy Online uses NWEA MAP testing three times per year, built directly into regular coursework. This satisfies the TEFA annual assessment requirement. Other approved assessments include STAAR, ACT, SAT, PSAT, and more.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
