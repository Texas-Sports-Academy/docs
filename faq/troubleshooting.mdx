Most enrollment and portal issues have straightforward fixes. Work through the relevant section below before reaching out — many common problems are resolved in minutes. If you do need help, the contacts and links below will get you to the right person quickly.

<Note>
  **Key support contacts:**
  - **Texas Sports Academy Online:** [online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com)
  - **Odyssey portal support:** [help.tx@withodyssey.com](mailto:help.tx@withodyssey.com) — CC [arpan.gupta@alpha.school](mailto:arpan.gupta@alpha.school) on any Odyssey emails
  - **Odyssey live chat:** [Open a support ticket](https://support.withodyssey.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=43962171255707) — click the green chat button, select **Parent**, then **Texas Education Freedom Accounts Program**
  - **Live Q&A webinars:** [sportsacademy.school/home/events](https://sportsacademy.school/home/events)
</Note>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I'm having trouble logging into the Odyssey portal.">
    Start by resetting your password at [auth.withodyssey.com/realms/odyssey/login-actions/reset-credentials](https://auth.withodyssey.com/realms/odyssey/login-actions/reset-credentials).

    If that doesn't resolve the issue, email [help.tx@withodyssey.com](mailto:help.tx@withodyssey.com) and CC [arpan.gupta@alpha.school](mailto:arpan.gupta@alpha.school).

    For immediate live help, use Odyssey's chat support: [open a ticket here](https://support.withodyssey.com/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=43962171255707), click the green chat button, select **Parent**, then **Texas Education Freedom Accounts Program**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My school selection keeps resetting to 'no school selected'.">
    This is a known glitch in the Odyssey portal. Email [help.tx@withodyssey.com](mailto:help.tx@withodyssey.com) describing what's happening — include which child is affected and what you're seeing on screen — and CC [arpan.gupta@alpha.school](mailto:arpan.gupta@alpha.school). The Odyssey team will resolve it on the back end.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My application status says 'Pending'. Is something wrong?">
    No — **Pending** is completely normal on the Odyssey portal. No action is needed on your part while your application is in this state.

    Here's what the other statuses mean:

    | Status | What it means |
    |---|---|
    | **Eligible** | Meets the initial criteria — does not guarantee funding |
    | **Under Review** | A manual check is needed before a decision is made |
    | **Ineligible** | Did not meet the program criteria |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I confirmed my address but can't order the MacBook.">
    Two things must be in place before you can order the MacBook:

    1. **Enrollment is paid** — or, for voucher families, your Odyssey school selection is confirmed. Note that a $0 balance alone is not enough; your Odyssey selection must also be confirmed.
    2. **Your child has a chosen start date.**

    To complete payment or check your status, visit the [enrollment checklist](https://sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checklist). To set or update a start date, go to the [preferences step](https://sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checklist?step=preferences).

    If you have multiple students enrolled, each child needs their own confirmed start date and completed payment before their MacBook can be ordered.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm getting a payment error during tuition checkout.">
    Banks often add extra verification (called 3D Secure) for larger online transactions. You may be redirected to your bank's website or app to approve the charge — this is normal and not a sign that something went wrong.

    First, check [your checkout page](https://sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checkout) to see whether the payment already went through before trying again. If the payment didn't process and the error keeps occurring, email [online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My child's enrollment shows as 'ineligible'.">
    The most common causes are:

    1. **The application hasn't been accepted yet by admissions** — decisions are usually made the same day.
    2. **Date of birth or grade level is missing from the profile** — update your child's profile at [sportsacademy.school/home/profile](https://sportsacademy.school/home/profile).
    3. **No application is on file** — submit an application at [online.sportsacademy.school/apply](https://online.sportsacademy.school/apply).

    If none of these apply to your situation, email [online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com) with your child's name and the details of what you're seeing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="A partner facility I contacted didn't know about Texas Sports Academy.">
    Flag it in the community or bring it up at a [live webinar](https://sportsacademy.school/home/events). The partnerships team will follow up directly with that facility to make sure they're fully informed.

    In the meantime, email [online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com) and the team can point you to other partner locations near you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I speak with someone at Texas Sports Academy?">
    The best way to get your questions answered by the team is to join a **live Q&A webinar** at [sportsacademy.school/home/events](https://sportsacademy.school/home/events). These are held regularly and cover a wide range of topics.

    If you need dedicated one-on-one time, you can book a 30-minute call:
    - **English:** [Book a call](https://calendly.com/d/cvkj-nyf-8w6/30-minute-call-with-texas-sports-academy-online)
    - **Spanish:** [Reservar una llamada](https://calendly.com/brandon-young-texassportsacademy/30min)
  </Accordion>

  </Accordion>
