Enrolling in Texas Sports Academy Online is designed to be straightforward and low-pressure. The application is free, takes only a few minutes, and places no obligation on your family. You can pause or withdraw at any point before your start date simply by sending an email. This page walks you through every step from first application to first day of school.

<Note>
  There are **two separate applications** for Texas Sports Academy Online families: (1) the **school enrollment application** described on this page, and (2) the **TEFA Education Savings Account (ESA) voucher application**, which is administered by the State of Texas through the Odyssey platform. The TEFA voucher application window for the 2025–2026 school year closed on **March 31, 2026**. Completing the school application does not automatically enroll you in—or guarantee approval for—the voucher program.
</Note>

<Note>
  Applying is not a commitment. Every step of the enrollment process is non-binding. If you decide Texas Sports Academy Online is not the right fit, simply email [online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com) to withdraw—no fees, no penalties.
</Note>

## Enrollment Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit Your Application">
    Start at **[online.sportsacademy.school/apply](https://online.sportsacademy.school/apply)**. The application is free and takes just a few minutes to complete. Submitting it reserves your spot in the application queue and carries zero obligation. You'll receive a confirmation once your application status moves to **Submitted**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the Enrollment Commitment">
    Once your application is reviewed, you'll be asked to sign the Enrollment Commitment at **[sportsacademy.school/home/contract](https://sportsacademy.school/home/contract)**. This document confirms your intent to enroll—it is not a binding contract. You can still withdraw at any time before your start date with no consequence.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Admissions Review">
    The admissions team reviews your application, typically on the **same day** it is received. You'll be notified when your status changes to **Accepted**. If any information is missing, someone will reach out directly to help you complete your file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pay Tuition">
    Proceed to checkout at **[sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checkout](https://sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checkout)** to select your payment plan and submit your first payment.

    | Payment Plan | Cost |
    |---|---|
    | Monthly (10 payments) | $1,100 / month ($11,000 / year) |
    | Quarterly | $2,600 / quarter (saves $600 / year) |

    <Note>
      If your family has been **approved for a TEFA ESA voucher**, tuition is paid directly from your Education Savings Account through the Odyssey platform. You pay nothing out of pocket. Your voucher funds are deposited by the State of Texas into an ESA you own—the school is simply listed as an approved provider.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Your Start Date and Training Facility">
    Visit **[sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checklist?step=preferences](https://sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checklist?step=preferences)** to choose your start date and select a partner training facility near you.

    | Term | Start Date |
    |---|---|
    | Summer Early-Start | **June 29, 2026** (every Monday through end of July) |
    | Fall 2026 | **August 18, 2026** |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Enrollment Paperwork">
    Finish all required enrollment documents at **[sportsacademy.school/online/paperwork](https://sportsacademy.school/online/paperwork)**. This includes academic records, health forms, and any state-required enrollment documentation. Once paperwork is complete and tuition is confirmed, your status moves to **Enrolled**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Application Status Guide

As you move through the process, your application will show one of three statuses.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Submitted">
    Your application has been received and is in the queue for admissions review. No action is needed from you at this stage—expect to hear back the same day in most cases.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Accepted">
    Admissions has reviewed and approved your application. You're ready to sign the Enrollment Commitment and move forward with the remaining steps.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Enrolled">
    All steps are complete: commitment signed, tuition arranged, paperwork submitted. You have a confirmed seat for your chosen start date.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Your MacBook

Every enrolled student receives a school-issued Apple MacBook shipped directly to their home before the start date.

- **Fall 2026 families:** Your MacBook ships closer to your August 18 start date.
- **Summer Early-Start families:** Laptops begin shipping the **week of June 22, 2026**.

Confirm or update your shipping address at **[sportsacademy.school/home/laptop](https://sportsacademy.school/home/laptop)** as soon as possible to avoid delays.

## Enrollment Checklist and Community

Need a bird's-eye view of where you stand? Visit your **[enrollment checklist](https://sportsacademy.school/home/enrollment/checklist)** at any time to see which steps are complete and what still needs attention.

Once enrolled, join the student and family community at **[sportsacademy.school/home/start](https://sportsacademy.school/home/start)** to connect with other families, get announcements, and hit the ground running before day one.

## Questions or Need to Withdraw?

Reach the enrollment team by email at [online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com). If you decide to withdraw at any point before your start date, a single email to that address is all it takes—no forms, no fees, no friction.
