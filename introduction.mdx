Texas Sports Academy Online is a virtual private school built for student-athletes and motivated learners in grades 3–12. Your child completes a full academic day in approximately two focused hours using personalized learning software, then spends the rest of the day training, pursuing passions, or spending time with family. Sports training at a partner facility of your choice is included in tuition — it's a core part of the program, not an afterthought.

## What Makes Texas Sports Academy Online Different

Most schools ask athletes to squeeze training in around a 6–8 hour school day. Texas Sports Academy Online flips that model. Academics happen first — efficiently, in about two hours — and the rest of the day belongs to your child.

## Who Is This Program For?

Texas Sports Academy Online works for any motivated learner in grades 3–12. While many families join because of competitive sports schedules, the flexible 2-hour day benefits everyone — including students passionate about art, music, entrepreneurship, dance, or any other pursuit.

<Note>
  Texas Sports Academy Online is a **virtual private school**, not a homeschool program. Your child receives official transcripts, GPA, class rank, and a private school diploma. The school is fully Cognia accredited and listed as "Texas Sports Academy Virtual" in the Cognia portal.
</Note>
