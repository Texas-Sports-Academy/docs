## App Playbook

## Detailed App Continuum with Hole Filling Apps – 2HR App Continuum (Session 2)

### Reading

- **Pre-K** — [Mentava Basics](/learning-apps/mentava-basics) (2XP per lesson)
- **Grades K–1** — [Alpha Reading Fluency](/learning-apps/alpha-reading-fluency)\*\* \+ [Anton](/learning-apps/anton) (Hole Filling)
- **Grade 2** — [Alpha Reading Fluency](/learning-apps/alpha-reading-fluency)\*\* \+ [MobyMax](/learning-apps/mobymax) (Hole Filling)
- **Grades 3–6** — [AlphaRead](/learning-apps/alpharead) (XP per article as shown in Timeback; at 80% accuracy)
- **Grades 7–8** — [AlphaRead](/learning-apps/alpharead) \+ [MobyMax](/learning-apps/mobymax) (at 80% accuracy)
- **Grades 9–12** — [AlphaRead](/learning-apps/alpharead)

### Language

- **Grades 1–2** — [Lalilo](/learning-apps/lalilo) (3XP per lesson part)
- **Grades 3–5** — [MobyMax](/learning-apps/mobymax) (5XP per lesson with 80% accuracy; 2XP per lesson test with 100% accuracy) \+ [Freckle](/learning-apps/freckle) (Hole Filling)
- **Grades 6–7** — [Egumpp](/learning-apps/egumpp) (XP depends on length of the lesson\*) \+ [Freckle](/learning-apps/freckle) (Hole Filling)
- **Grade 8** — [MobyMax](/learning-apps/mobymax) \+ [Freckle](/learning-apps/freckle) (Hole Filling)
- **Grades 9–10** — [Freckle](/learning-apps/freckle) (Hole Filling)
- **Grades 11–12** — [AP Language / AP Literature](/learning-apps/ap-language-literature)

### Math

- **Pre-K** — [Happy Numbers](/learning-apps/happy-numbers) (3XP per lesson)
- **Grades K–2** — [Zearn](/learning-apps/zearn) (25 XP per lesson)
- **Grade 3** — [Zearn](/learning-apps/zearn) (25 XP per lesson) \+ [AlphaMath](/learning-apps/alphamath) (Hole-Filling)
- **Grades 4–5** — [Math Academy](/learning-apps/math-academy) \+ Grade-level Supplement (G4 / G5) \+ [AlphaMath](/learning-apps/alphamath) (Hole-Filling)
- **Grades 6–8** — [Math Academy](/learning-apps/math-academy) – Prealgebra \+ Grade-level Supplement (G6 / G7 / G8) \+ [Edia](/learning-apps/edia) (Hole-Filling)
- **Grades 9–12** — [Math Academy](/learning-apps/math-academy) (exactly as mathacademy reports it)

### Writing

- **Grades 6–8** — [AlphaWrite](/learning-apps/alphawrite) (15XP per activity in Sentences & Paragraphs; 125XP per completed essay)

### Science

- **Grades 4–5** — [MobyMax](/learning-apps/mobymax) (13XP per lesson with 80% accuracy)
- **Grades 6–8** — [Nice Academy (MS)](/learning-apps/nice-academy) — XP per lesson as shown in Timeback (Full XP at 80% accuracy) \+ [MobyMax](/learning-apps/mobymax) (Hole Filling)
- **Grades 9–10** — [Edmentum HS Biology](/learning-apps/edmentum-hs-biology)

### Vocab

- **Grades 4–5** — [VocabLoco](/learning-apps/vocabloco) (5 XP for each activity with 80% accuracy)
- **Grades 6–8** — [Membean](/learning-apps/membean) (0.75 XP for every new word; 0.33 XP for every question answered correctly at 80% accuracy)
- **Grades 9–10** — [VocabLoco](/learning-apps/vocabloco) (Hole Filling)

### Fast Math

- **Pre-K–Grade 2** — [Alpha Numbers](/learning-apps/alpha-numbers) (1XP per active minute at 80% accuracy)
- **Grades 3–8** — [Math Raiders](/learning-apps/math-raiders) (1XP per active minute at 80% accuracy)

\*In eGumpp, each lesson has several components (XP is awarded individually for the completion of each component).\
\*\*Variable XP (3–5) for recording stage, depending on story length. 5XP for Comprehension Quiz, if ≥ 80%.
