This page expands on [the TEFA Voucher overview](/funding/tefa-voucher) with the full eligibility rules and the state's four-tier priority system.

<Warning>
  The TEFA application window **closed on March 31, 2026**. This is reference material for families who already applied. New families can enroll at the self-pay rate and join the community at [sportsacademy.school/home/start](https://sportsacademy.school/home/start). The program is expected to reopen for 2027–2028.
</Warning>

## Who Is Eligible

To enroll at Texas Sports Academy Online with TEFA funding, a student must be:

- In **grades 3–12** (Texas Sports Academy Online serves grades 3–12 only)
- A **Texas resident**, eligible to attend Texas public school
- Located **anywhere in Texas** — the program is fully virtual

<Note>
  TEFA itself also funds eligible **prekindergarten** students (ages 3–5) under separate qualifying categories, but those must use approved pre-K providers. Texas Sports Academy Online enrolls grades 3–12.
</Note>

### Age Requirements

A child must meet Texas public school age requirements by the **September 1, 2026** cutoff:

| Grade level | Age requirement |
| --- | --- |
| Prekindergarten | At least 3 and no older than 5 |
| Kindergarten–12th grade | Between 5 and 25 years old |

### Who Can Apply

Only a child's **parent or legal guardian** who is a Texas resident may apply — this includes a biological or adoptive parent, managing or possessory conservator, legal guardian, custodian, or another person with legal authority to act for the child.

## Priority Groups

There is **no income limit** to apply, but the state allocates awards using four priority groups based on household income (relative to the Federal Poverty Level, FPL) and disability status:

| Priority | Who it covers |
| --- | --- |
| **Priority 1** | A child with a disability in a household at or below **500% FPL** |
| **Priority 2** | A child in a household at or below **200% FPL** |
| **Priority 3** | A child in a household between **200% and 500% FPL** |
| **Priority 4** | A child in a household **above 500% FPL** (prior public-school students prioritized within this group; capped at **20%** of total program funding) |

Applications submitted any time within the open window were treated equally — applying earlier gave no advantage.

### Federal Poverty Level Thresholds

Household size is based on the dependents claimed on your 2024 or 2025 federal tax return.

| Household size | 200% FPL | 500% FPL |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | \$31,920 | \$79,800 |
| 2 | \$43,280 | \$108,200 |
| 3 | \$54,640 | \$136,600 |
| 4 | \$66,000 | \$165,000 |
| 5 | \$77,360 | \$193,400 |
| 6 | \$88,720 | \$221,800 |
| 7 | \$100,080 | \$250,200 |
| 8 | \$111,440 | \$278,600 |
| Each additional person | \+\$11,360 | \+\$28,400 |

## Selection, Siblings & Renewal

- **Selection:** Higher-priority groups are selected first. If demand exceeds funding within a group, the state runs a lottery within that group.
- **Sibling acceptance:** If any child in a household is accepted during an application period, all eligible siblings who applied **in the same window** are automatically accepted — regardless of each sibling's own priority tier. Siblings must still be eligible (ages 3–25, Texas resident).
- **Renewal:** Once accepted, funding continues annually through high school graduation. Parents submit an annual notice of intent — no new lottery or reapplication is required.

## Active-Duty Military Families

Active-duty military families moving to Texas may participate as long as the family will be in Texas **before the start of the 2026–27 school year**. Enter a Texas-based address on the application (your duty station address is acceptable if you don't yet have housing — update it in Odyssey before the school year), and upload your **active-duty orders** for verification.

## Award Timeline & Next Steps

For families who applied before the deadline:

| Milestone | Date |
| --- | --- |
| First-round (Tier 1) award notices | Late April 2026 |
| Most Tier 2 notices \+ waitlist positions | Late April 2026 |
| Initial funding available (at least 25%) | July 1, 2026 |
| Confirm enrollment / final school selection | July 15, 2026 |

- **Check your status** anytime in the [Odyssey dashboard](https://withodyssey.com/en/texas-esa/dashboard) under Application History.
- **Waitlisted?** That doesn't mean you won't be funded — families move up as awarded families opt out or miss the July 15 confirmation deadline.
- **Disagree with a decision?** File an appeal with Odyssey within **30 days** of your notification via the [TEFA Parent Appeals Process](https://support.withodyssey.com/hc/en-us/articles/49194996729115-TEFA-Parent-Appeals-Process).
