Self-pay is the standard enrollment path for families who did not apply for the TEFA voucher, are awaiting a decision, or simply prefer to enroll now without waiting. The academic program, sports training, and every included benefit are **identical** whether you pay tuition directly or through an Education Savings Account (ESA) — the only difference is how the bill gets paid.

<Note>
  The $10,400 tuition described on this page is for **Texas Sports Academy Online only**. Physical campus programs (such as Lake Travis) are separate schools with different tuition structures ranging from approximately $25,000–$50,000/year depending on location and financial aid. This page does not apply to those programs.
</Note>

## Tuition at a Glance

Texas Sports Academy Online tuition is **$10,400 per year**. You are never asked to pay the full year upfront — tuition is spread across monthly or quarterly payments.

| Plan | Payment Amount | Payments | Annual Total | Annual Savings |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly** | $1,100 / month | 10 payments | $11,000 | — |
| **Quarterly** | $2,600 / quarter | 4 payments | $10,400 | **$600** |

The Quarterly plan locks in the base tuition rate and saves $600 compared to the Monthly plan. Both plans provide access to the full program from day one.

## What's Included in Tuition

Your tuition covers everything your student needs — there are no add-on fees for the core program.

- **Academics** — 2 Hour Learning adaptive software platform
- **Sports training** — in-person sessions at a Texas Sports Academy partner facility
- **Apple MacBook** — provided for educational use while enrolled
- **1:1 on-demand tutoring** — available whenever your student needs extra help
- **NIL life skills courses** — name, image, and likeness preparation for student-athletes
- **College recruiting support** — guidance and resources for the recruiting process
- **Parent dashboard** — real-time visibility into your student's academic progress

**AP coursework** is included in the program. AP exam fees are separate and paid directly to College Board.

## Early-Start and Fall 2026 Dates

Self-pay families can begin enrollment before the official Fall 2026 semester. If you start before Fall 2026, tuition is billed at **$1,100/month** on the Monthly plan from your start date. The official Fall 2026 semester begins **August 18, 2026**.

## The $8,000 Base Plan

A Base Plan is available at $8,000/year. It includes the same academic program but does **not** include in-person sports training at a partner facility. The Base Plan is not listed in the standard enrollment flow — contact the Texas Sports Academy team directly to request it.

## Enrolling While a Voucher Decision Is Pending

If you applied for the TEFA voucher before the March 31, 2026 deadline and haven't received a decision yet, you can still enroll as a self-pay student now and continue your education without interruption.

If your voucher is later approved, the ESA funding applies **going forward from the approval date**. Tuition payments already made as self-pay are not refunded, credited, or offset. There are no refunds, rebates, or partial credits for prior months — the program simply transitions to ESA-funded billing at the point of approval.

The TEFA program is expected to run annually. If you are not approved in the current cycle, the next application window is anticipated in **early 2027** for the 2027–2028 school year.

## Withdrawing from the Program

There is no long-term contract and no withdrawal penalty. If you need to leave the program, email **[online@texassportsacademy.com](mailto:online@texassportsacademy.com)** to begin the withdrawal process.

**Laptop return:** The Apple MacBook provided at enrollment is for educational use while your student is enrolled — it is not yours to keep and cannot be sold. Upon withdrawal, you must return the laptop. Texas Sports Academy covers the cost of return shipping.
