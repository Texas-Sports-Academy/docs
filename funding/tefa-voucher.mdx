The Texas Education Freedom Account (TEFA) is a state program that deposits $10,474 per child, per year into an Education Savings Account (ESA) that you own — giving Texas families a way to fund private school tuition with state money, not out of pocket. If you applied before the deadline and were approved, that funding covers 100% of Texas Sports Academy Online's $10,400 tuition with money left over.

<Warning>
  The TEFA application window **closed on March 31, 2026**. No late applications or extensions are being accepted. The program is expected to continue annually — the next application window is anticipated in early 2027 for the 2027–2028 school year.
</Warning>

## What Is the TEFA Voucher?

TEFA stands for **Texas Education Freedom Account**. It is a state program — not a loan, not taxable income, and not a personal cash payment — that places $10,474 per eligible child into an ESA managed through the **Odyssey platform**. Tuition is paid directly from the ESA to the school. You never handle cash, and you never pay it back.

Because the annual ESA amount ($10,474) exceeds Texas Sports Academy Online's annual tuition ($10,400), **families who were approved owe nothing out of pocket** for tuition.

## Who Qualifies?

To be eligible for TEFA, a student must:

- Be a **Texas resident**
- Be eligible to attend a Texas public school
- Be in **grades Pre-K through 12** (Texas Sports Academy Online serves grades 3–12)

There is **no income limit** to apply. However, the state uses a priority system when allocating awards:

| Priority Tier | Who It Covers |
|---|---|
| Tier 1 | Students with disabilities and lower-income families |
| Tier 2 | Middle-income families |
| Tier 3 | Higher-income families |

All applications submitted within the open window are treated equally — applying earlier in the window gives no advantage over applying on the last day.

## If You Already Applied

Most Tier 1 and Tier 2 notification letters went out in **late April / early May 2026**. To check your current status:

1. Visit your Odyssey dashboard: [withodyssey.com/en/texas-esa/dashboard](https://withodyssey.com/en/texas-esa/dashboard)
2. Look under **Application History** for your award or waitlist status.

**Key dates to keep in mind:**

| Milestone | Date |
|---|---|
| Initial funding available (at least 25%) | July 1, 2026 |
| School selection — initial deadline | June 1, 2026 |
| Confirm enrollment / final school selection deadline | July 15, 2026 |

If your award is confirmed, you must select Texas Sports Academy as your school and confirm enrollment **by July 15, 2026**, or your award may be forfeited.

## Post-Approval: How Funding Flows to Tuition

After you receive an award, three steps activate the flow of ESA funds to your tuition. Complete them in order.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Opt In and Select Texas Sports Academy">
    Log in to your Odyssey portal. Go to **Student Profiles → your child's name → Select School**. Search for **Texas Sports Academy** and select the address below. This opts your ESA into paying tuition for this school.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Texas Sports Academy Enters the Tuition Amount">
    Once you've selected the school, Texas Sports Academy will enter the tuition amount ($10,400) on its end in Odyssey. You don't need to do anything during this step — we handle it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You Confirm the Tuition in Odyssey">
    You'll receive a prompt in Odyssey to confirm the tuition amount entered by the school. Once you confirm, the ESA funds are released to Texas Sports Academy and your enrollment is financially secured.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  **Which address to select in Odyssey:** When searching for Texas Sports Academy in the school dropdown, select **4402 Hudson Bend Rd, Austin, TX 78734**. Use this address even if you don't live in Austin — it is the official business address for the Online program and is the correct selection for all virtual students.
</Note>

## Voucher vs. Homeschool: Choose "Private School"

Texas Sports Academy Online is a **virtual private school**, not a homeschool program. In the Odyssey portal, you must select **Private School** — not Homeschool — when setting up your student's profile. Selecting the wrong option has significant financial consequences:

- **Private School setting** → $10,474 ESA (covers full tuition)
- **Homeschool setting** → ~$2,000 ESA only

<Warning>
  After March 31, 2026, you **cannot switch from Homeschool up to Private School** — that is a one-way door. You can switch *down* from Private School to Homeschool, but not the other direction. If you're unsure which setting you selected, check your Odyssey portal immediately.
</Warning>

## If You're on the Waitlist

Being on the waitlist does not mean you won't receive funding. Waitlisted families move up automatically if awarded families opt out or fail to confirm their enrollment by **July 15, 2026**. Log in to your Odyssey portal regularly to monitor your position and watch for email notifications.

## If You Believe a Mistake Was Made

If you think your application was processed incorrectly, you have the right to appeal **within 30 days of your notification**. Submit your appeal through Odyssey's official process:
