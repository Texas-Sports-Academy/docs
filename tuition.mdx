Texas Sports Academy Online operates on a single all-inclusive tuition that covers every component of your student's program—academics, training, technology, tutoring, and more. There are no surprise add-ons and no separate invoices for the parts that matter most. This page explains exactly what tuition covers, how you can pay, and how the TEFA Education Savings Account voucher works for approved families.

## What's Included in Tuition

The full program tuition is **$10,400 per year**. Every item listed below is covered—nothing requires a separate purchase or subscription.

| What's Included | Details |
|---|---|
| **Personalized Academic Software** | 2 Hour Learning adaptive platform, covering all core subjects for grades 3–12 |
| **In-Person Sports Training** | Daily training at a vetted local partner facility—no separate membership fee |
| **Apple MacBook** | School-issued laptop shipped to your home before your start date |
| **Human 1:1 Tutoring** | On-demand live video tutoring with a qualified educator, unlimited |
| **NIL Life Skills Courses** | Public speaking, financial literacy, grit, and more—built into the curriculum |
| **College Recruiting Support** | Guidance and resources to navigate the college athletic recruiting process |
| **Parent Dashboard** | Daily visibility into your student's academic progress and pace |
| **Live Guide Sessions** | Daily Zoom sessions Monday–Friday with a credentialed educator (3 time slots) |

<Note>
  The $10,400 full-program tuition is specific to **Texas Sports Academy Online**. It does not apply to Texas Sports Academy's physical campus programs. If your family is primarily interested in academics without in-person partner facility training, a base plan at $8,000/year is available—ask the admissions team for details.
</Note>

## The TEFA Education Savings Account (ESA) Voucher

The Texas Education Freedom Act (TEFA) creates an **Education Savings Account (ESA)**—an account owned by *you*, the parent—into which the State of Texas deposits **$10,474** per eligible student per year. That ESA balance is then used to pay tuition directly to Texas Sports Academy Online through the **Odyssey platform**. You never handle the funds as cash, and the money goes directly from your ESA to the school.

<Note>
  The TEFA ESA voucher is **not a grant, not a reimbursement, and not taxable income**. It is an Education Savings Account funded by the state and owned by you. Approval is not guaranteed—families must apply during the open application window and meet eligibility requirements set by the State of Texas.
</Note>

### How the Voucher Covers Your Tuition

<Steps>
  <Step title="State Deposits Funds Into Your ESA">
    Once approved, the State of Texas deposits $10,474 into your Education Savings Account on the Odyssey platform. The account is yours—you are the account holder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="You Pay Tuition Through Odyssey">
    Log in to your Odyssey dashboard and direct payment to Texas Sports Academy Online. The funds transfer from your ESA to the school. **You pay nothing out of pocket.**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tuition Covers the Full Program">
    Your $10,400 tuition is now settled. Because sports training is included in tuition, your ESA effectively covers in-person training—but the chain is: **ESA → tuition → training**. The voucher does not pay for sports training directly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Voucher Application Window

The TEFA voucher application window for the **2025–2026 school year closed on March 31, 2026**. Families approved before that date pay nothing out of pocket for the coming school year.

For families who did not apply or were not approved in time:

- Enroll as a **self-pay family** at $10,400/year using the monthly or quarterly plan.
- The voucher program is expected to continue annually. The next application window is anticipated to open in **early 2027** for the 2027–2028 school year.

Check your voucher application status or manage your ESA at **[withodyssey.com/en/texas-esa/dashboard](https://withodyssey.com/en/texas-esa/dashboard)**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does tuition include sports training at a partner facility?">
    Yes. In-person sports training at a local partner facility is included in the $10,400 full-program tuition. You will not receive a separate invoice for training.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can the TEFA voucher pay for sports training directly?">
    No. The voucher funds your Education Savings Account, which you use to pay tuition to Texas Sports Academy Online. Tuition in turn covers all program components, including sports training. The voucher does not pay training providers directly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the voucher money considered income?">
    No. TEFA ESA funds are not taxable income. The money is deposited by the state into an education savings account and is restricted to approved educational expenses.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if I was not approved for the TEFA voucher?">
    You can still enroll as a self-pay family. The monthly plan ($1,100/month) and quarterly plan ($2,600/quarter) make the full program accessible without a voucher. The next voucher application window is expected to open in early 2027.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there a lower-cost option without in-person training?">
    Yes. A base plan at **$8,000/year** includes the full academic program, MacBook, tutoring, NIL courses, and recruiting support—but does not include in-person sports training at a partner facility. Virtual training options are available under this plan. Contact the admissions team to learn more.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is the $10,400 tuition the same at physical campuses?">
    No. The $10,400 all-inclusive tuition applies exclusively to **Texas Sports Academy Online**. Physical campus programs have different pricing structures.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
